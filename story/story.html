I visited Killarney Mountain Lodge with my family in August of 2023. It took around 5 hours to get there and we stayed for a week. On the trip we hiked the Tar Vat trail to the Killarney Lighthouse, went kayaking, canoeing, and swimming in the Killarney Channel, and often ate at the Sportsman's Inn.
